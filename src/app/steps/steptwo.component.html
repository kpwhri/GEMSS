Choose your medicine

img for Pharmacy

Using an FDA–approved medicine will double your chances of quitting smoking and staying quit if you use it as directed. Take some time to research your options.

Depending on the medicine you use, you will either start using it on your Quit Date or a week before, so plan ahead

Talk to your doctor to learn which of these medicines is right for you. Jot down any questions you have for your doctor in your GEMS Journal, so you'll have them handy.
