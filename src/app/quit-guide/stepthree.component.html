Choose your quit date

After you've chosen which medicine you'll use, you're ready to choose a Quit Date.

Here's how to choose the perfect day to stop smoking:

  • Pick a date between 2 days and 2 weeks in the future. You need a little time to prepare for this day, but not too much time or you might change your mind.
  • Choose a date when you don't have a lot of other things to do. Weekends often work best.
  • Mark it on your calendar and set a reminder 2 days before.

Once you do this, you're ready to get prepared for your Quit Date (Step 4).
