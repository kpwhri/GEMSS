Quit Smoking!

After your Quit Date, it's important that you don't smoke — even a puff. But if you do, don't give in and give up!

Follow these steps to ensure your slip does not become a relapse.

Graphic
STOP!

Don't finish the cigarette.

Put it out and walk away.

Graphic
RECOMMIT

to quitting smoking

  • Throw away any cigarettes you have.
  • Keep taking your stop-smoking medicine (or get one, if you haven't yet).
  • Set a new Quit Date right away.
  • Revisit Steps1–5 of this program for more help.
Graphic
ANALYZE

the situation

Why did you smoke? What tripped you up? What can you do differently next time you're tempted, so you don't smoke in the future?

  • Write down your answers in your GEMS Journal, so you don't forget what you've learned.
Graphic
BE COMPASSIONATE

Don't beat yourself up

Don't beat yourself up. It's a slip, not a relapse. You're learning how to be a non-smoker again. As long as you get back on track, it's not a failure, just part of the process.
