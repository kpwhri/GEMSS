Risks of smoking

Did you know …

  • Smoking can cause:
    • Sexual impotence
    • Wrinkles and facial aging
    • Gum disease and cavities
    • Osteoporosis in women
  • Children of smokers have more lung and breathing problems
  • Smokers miss more work
  • Smokers take longer to recover from illness
  • Smoking can worsen depression and anxiety
  • Smoking can cause some medications to not work as well

And smokers are

  • 10 times more likely to develop cancer of the lung, head and neck, bladder or cervix
  • 20 times more likely to develop lung disease
  • Up to 5 times more likely to develop heart disease, stroke and blood circulation problems

You can lessen these risks by quitting smoking.
