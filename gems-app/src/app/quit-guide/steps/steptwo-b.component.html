Varenicline

Varenicline (brand names Chantix and Champix) is one of the three FDA-approved stop-smoking medicines. It is only available with a presciption,

Who can it?

Varenicline is safe for most, but may not be the best choice for everyone. Talk to your doctor to find out if it is right for you.

How does it work?

Varenicline blocks the pleasant effects of smoking in the brain. This makes it easier to stop smoking.

How do I use it?

You will start taking varenicline one week before your Quit Date. Take it the same time each day and pair it with other activities to help you remember, like with breakfast and befor bed. Consider setting a reminder alarm on your phone.

Take your pill(s) with food and a full glass of water to reduce the side effects.

Follow your doctor's instructions, but most people need to stay on this medicine for 12 weeks. Be sure to re-order your refill before you run out.

What are the side effects?

Common side effects include nausea, sleep problems, constipation, gas and vomiting. More serious side effects have been reported. Talk to your doctor to learn more.
