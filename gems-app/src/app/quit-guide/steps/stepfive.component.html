Quit Smoking!

Your quit date has finally arrived. Here are some tips for getting through the day:

  • Be sure to use your stop-smoking medicine.
  • Avoid places where people are smoking
  • Avoid things that will make you want to smoke (e.g., coffee, beer). This is not forever, but at least a few weeks.
  • Go for a walk.
  • Think about all of the reasons not smoking is a good idea.
  • Throw away any last cigarettes you still have — you know, the ones you weren't ready to part with last night.
  • If you have a craving, remind yourself that it will pass. Don't give in.
  • Practice your skill-power!
  • Think of this as your first day (of many to come) as a new non-smoker!

If you're like most people, this day will be the hardest, so hang in there and remember that it will get easier.

Step 6 can help you if you slip and get off course. We suggest you review this now and then refer to it again when things get tough.
